🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

 🔹 Business Problem

Blinkit wants to analyze its sales performance across different outlet types, item categories, and locations to improve revenue and operational efficiency.

 🔹 Objective

To analyze sales data and identify key trends, high-performing products, and areas of improvement.

🔹 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning
* Data Visualization

🔹 Key Metrics

* Total Sales: ~$1.2M
* Average Sales: $141
* Number of Items: 8523
* Average Rating: 3.9

🔹 Key Insights

* Tier 3 outlets generate the highest revenue compared to other locations
* Fruits and Snacks categories contribute significantly to total sales
* Low-fat products have higher sales contribution than regular items
* Supermarket Type 1 has the highest total sales among outlet types

 🔹 Business Recommendations

* Focus expansion in Tier 3 cities to maximize revenue
* Increase stock of high-performing categories like fruits and snacks
* Improve performance of low-performing outlet types
* Optimize product mix based on customer preferences

 🔹 Conclusion

This dashboard helps in understanding sales distribution, product performance, and outlet efficiency, enabling better business decisions.
